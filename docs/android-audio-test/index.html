Android Audio Diagnostics

Build v49 — do the tests in order. When you're done, tap Copy results at the bottom and send it to Todd.

Before you start: turn media volume all the way up, and turn Battery Saver off.
Test 1 — Sound check (screen stays on)
Tap Run and keep the screen on. Listen for the beeps, then tap whether you heard them.
—
Test 2 — Quick pocket check (about 35 seconds)
Tap Run. The moment you hear the first beep, press the power button to turn the screen off and slide the phone into your pocket. When the beeps stop, take it out and tap what you heard.
—
Test 3 — The main test (about 3 minutes)
Tap Run. When you hear the first beep, turn the screen off (power button) and keep the phone in your pocket for the full 3 minutes. Take it out and tap what you heard. If it comes back bad, turn Battery Saver off and run this one more time.
—
Test 4 — Real app check
Open the app (footer must say build v49): Try the Demo → Start Practice → Go Live. Tap which banner you saw below. Then lock the phone, pocket it 3+ minutes, and tap what you heard.
